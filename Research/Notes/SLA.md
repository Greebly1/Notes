Stereo Lithography Assembly
AKA
	Resin Printing

The Print Bed is flipped upside down. Each layer of the assembly is dunked into a liquid resin, and lasers solidify each layer.

**Pros**
- Higher quality
**Cons**
- Slower
- Usually smaller
- Resin is toxic and must be disposed of properly
