The data connector for [[SATA]] hard drives

7 Pins
Uses [[Differential Signaling]] to produce 1 full-duplex 'lane' out of the 4 data wires

| 1   | Ground                |
| --- | --------------------- |
| 2   | A+ (Transmit)         |
| 3   | A- (Transmit)         |
| 4   | Ground                |
| 5   | B- (Receive)          |
| 6   | B+ (Receive)          |
| 7   | Ground                |
| 8   | L shaped keying notch |
